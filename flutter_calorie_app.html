Flutter Calorie Tracker

Mobile app for tracking calorie intake

Functionality

A mobile app that tracks your daily calorie intakes.
Users set their own daily calorie goals and put their foods/meals with calories that they contain into the database through the UI.
They can add (and delete) food/meals throughout the day and track past daily intakes (which can also be deleted).

APP DEMO


WHAT WAS USED

  • Flutter | Framework
  • Dart | Programming Language
  • SQLite | For database purposes
